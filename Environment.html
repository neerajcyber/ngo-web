Environmental Initiatives

A cultural program and tree plantation drive was organized in Plasbani village of Jugsalai assembly constituency in collaboration with Bidhu Chandan Trust for Tribal Self Empowerment.

In collaboration with Shaheed Smark Samiti, students of Patmada Degree College, Jalla were honored with saplings followed by a tree plantation program.

A tree plantation program was organized in Telco, Barinagar with the support of Aadarsh Society.

A tree plantation program was conducted in Dibadih village (Rajnagar block) of Seraikela Kharsawan with the cooperation of Veer Shaheed Dibakisun Committee.
